From Farm to Cup

Slow, honest, small-batch

No shortcuts and no anonymous middlemen — just four steps we obsess over every single day.

01

Source

We travel to origin and buy directly from growers, paying well above the commodity price for quality and trust.

02

Roast

Small batches roasted fresh each morning on our 1950s drum roaster, profiled by ear and by nose.

03

Rest

Beans rest two to four days to settle, so every bag pours at its sweet, balanced peak.

04

Brew

Dialed in daily by our baristas, or shipped to your door in compostable bags within 48 hours of roasting.
