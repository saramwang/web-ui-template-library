Our Manifesto

WE PRESS RECORDS NOBODY ELSE WILL. WE BOOK ROOMS NOBODY ELSE CAN FILL. WE PAY ARTISTS FIRST AND ARGUE LATER.

Static Noise started in a basement with one tape duplicator and a busted PA. Seventeen years later we’ve put out 60+ releases and thrown one festival a year that never breaks even and never stops. If it’s too loud, too weird, or too honest for everyone else, send it to us.
