Our House

A small atelier with a long memory.

Founded above a quiet courtyard in Milan, Maison Éclat began with a single obsession: the way good fabric ages. Every garment we make starts as a length of cloth chosen by hand, then cut, draped and revised until it earns its place.

We release only two collections a year and keep our runs intentionally small. What you buy from us is meant to be repaired, not replaced — a wardrobe that grows softer and more yours with every wear.
