03 — Studio & Ethos

Restraint is a kind of generosity.

We design slowly and build to last — because a good building is one you stop noticing and simply begin to live inside.

Vonhaus is a sixteen-person studio led by partners who still draw by hand every morning. We take on a deliberately small number of commissions each year so that every project receives the attention it deserves.

Our work has been recognised by RIBA, Dezeen and the A+ Awards, but the recognition we care about most arrives quietly — years later, when a client tells us the house still feels right.
