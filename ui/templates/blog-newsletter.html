About Elena

I'm a writer living at the edge of a small harbor town. For fifteen years I wrote for other people — magazines, brands, a book that did fine. The Slow Letter is the thing I write only for the joy of it, and somehow it has become the thing people read most.

There is no course to buy, no funnel, no growth strategy. Just a fortnightly note from one desk to yours, and the quiet conviction that some things are worth doing slowly.

— Elena
