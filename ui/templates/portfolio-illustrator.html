About me

Hi, I'm Pip — I draw happy little worlds for a living.

For the past eight years I've been making characters squishy, gradients loud, and brand mascots impossible to forget. I work somewhere between 2D illustration and 3D motion, and I genuinely cannot stop fiddling with color.

When I'm not animating a bouncing blob, I'm probably teaching workshops, doodling on receipts, or naming my Blender files after snacks.
